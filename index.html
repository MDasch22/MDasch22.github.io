Resume

Projects

Listed below you'll find some of the projects I am currently working on. Please feel free to explore each one of the sites by simply clicking on the picture.

Instashow

Instashow is a solo full-stack pixel-perfect clone based on the popular social media platform, Instagram. This application provides a platform where users can interact with other users via posts/comments/likes/followers/ and the user update system. These features give the user a sense of personal customization in regards to what they are being displayed and how they choose to be displayed on the site.

Snacksy

Snacksy was a group full-stack project clone of the popular e-commerce site Etsy. This application provides an avenue where snackers across the world can come together to share their love for all snacks. Users will have the ability to add snacks to our database as well as leave their thoughts and ideas on that particular snack which will be viewed by all other users.

About

Hi, my name is Michael Dasch and the cutie next to my left is Leo! I was born on Yokosuka Military base located in Kanagawa Japan, where I spent a little over the first decade of my life. I am now currently living in San Diego, California looking for new opportunities in the software engineering world.

I am a Colorado Mesa University graduate, with a Bachelor of Science (B.S.) in Kinesiology. I was also a 4 year starter on the NCAA Division 2 Mens Lacrosse team where we won 3 conference championships, so I am well aware of what it takes for a team to be successful.

I am a recent App Academy SWE Bootcamp graduate, and throughout my time at App Academy, I've learned languages such as JavaScript, Python, HTML, and CSS. With those languages, I was also fortunate to learn new frameworks and tools needed in order to create a full stack application, such as React.js, Redux, Flask, Express, SQLAlchemy, Sequelize, PostgreSQL, GIT, and Heroku. I am well practiced in RESTful API's, Test Driven Development, and Object Oriented Programming with countless hours of pair-programming with fellow programmers.

In the Projects section, you'll find some of my personal web applications that I am currently working on, so please feel free to give them a look and let me know your thoughts. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact me by clicking on the "Contact" tab
